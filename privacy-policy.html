Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

Splash Studios ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting player privacy while maintaining secure, stable, and fair environments across all our online games and live services. This Privacy Policy details how data is explicitly generated, stored, and managed when you interact with our gaming software ecosystems.

1. The PlayFab Backend Architecture

To operate reliable multiplayer live-ops, track persistent accounts, and secure game-state synchronization, our software infrastructure operates via Microsoft PlayFab backend nodes. When connecting to our online services, your device transmits unique, hardware-level cryptographs or platform-assigned tokens to authenticate account integrity and authorize cloud save profile mapping.

2. Data Categories We Process

Our telemetry gathering protocols process data restricted strictly to administrative operation requirements. This scope contains:

  • In-Game Currency Balances: Logging server-verified ledger modifications for earned or purchased game currency balances to block database manipulation or unauthorized balance generation.
  • Virtual Purchases & Entitlements: Processing validation tokens and inventory catalogs to secure player items and confirm store purchases.
  • Moderation Logs & Account Suspensions: Tracking player identification states, anti-cheat hardware telemetry, reports, and ban histories to remove malicious entities or actors breaking fair-play policies.

3. Explicit Data Allocation

All recorded operational streams are used to run our software securely. This telemetry is handled to validate virtual inventories, process marketplace logic, check anti-cheat rules, and enforce necessary software-level suspensions or bans. This directly protects our virtual game economies from duplication exploits or unauthorized balance tampering.

4. Data Sharing & Confidentiality

Splash Studios does not lease, sell, distribute, or share user identification telemetry or account records with third-party tracking networks or programmatic advertising networks. Your data remains stored inside secure, access-controlled PlayFab partitions. Access to administrative dashboards is restricted to core development leads managing live software deployments or addressing active system tickets.

5. Account Erasure Rights

Players retain full authority to request data deletion or inspect individual metadata profiles bound to our remote storage cells at any point. To request account erasure or open a compliance ticket, please get in touch with our team through our official communications channels.
