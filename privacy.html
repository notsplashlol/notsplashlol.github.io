Privacy Policy

How we collect and use data when you play our games.

Last updated: June 2026

Note: this policy is a plain-language template describing our actual data practices. It isn't a substitute for legal advice — if your players are in regions with specific requirements (for example GDPR in the EU/UK, CCPA in California, or COPPA for younger players), have it reviewed by a qualified professional before relying on it as your final, compliant policy.

Splash Studios ("we," "us," or "our") makes VR games, including Starving Apes 2. This policy explains what information we collect when you play our games, and how we use PlayFab — our backend service provider — to manage accounts, in-game currency, purchases, and moderation.

1. Who We Are

Splash Studios is an independent game studio. This policy covers the games we publish and any backend services (such as PlayFab) that those games connect to. It does not cover third-party platforms you use alongside our games, such as the Meta Quest Store or Discord, which have their own separate privacy policies.

2. Information We Collect

  • Player & account identifiers — your platform account ID, a PlayFab ID generated for your account, and any display name or handle you choose.
  • Gameplay & progress data — save data, level progress, and in-game inventory.
  • Virtual currency & purchase data — your in-game currency balance and a record of purchases made through the platform store, reflected as entitlements in PlayFab. We do not collect or store your full payment card details — those are handled entirely by the platform store (for example, the Meta Quest Store).
  • Device & platform data — basic technical information like headset/platform type, app version, and diagnostic logs used to fix bugs and crashes.
  • Moderation data — records of reports, warnings, suspensions, or bans tied to your account, including the reason and duration.

3. How We Use PlayFab

PlayFab is a game backend platform operated by Microsoft. We use it to:

  • store and sync player profiles and save data across sessions;
  • manage virtual currency balances and in-game inventory;
  • record and reconcile purchases made through supported platform stores;
  • enforce account suspensions and bans for violations of our terms; and
  • run basic analytics so we can understand crashes and usage patterns and fix problems faster.

PlayFab acts as a data processor on our behalf. Microsoft's own privacy practices also apply to data it processes through PlayFab — you can review those at privacy.microsoft.com.

4. In-Game Currency & Purchases

Any virtual currency in our games has no real-world cash value and isn't redeemable for real money. Purchases of virtual currency or items are processed by the platform store you bought them through, and that store's refund policy applies — Splash Studios doesn't process payments or store card details directly. PlayFab simply keeps a record of what you've purchased and your current balance so your account stays accurate across sessions.

5. Bans & Moderation

We may suspend or ban accounts for reasons including cheating, exploiting bugs for unfair advantage, harassment, or other violations of our terms of service. When we do, PlayFab stores the ban status, the reason, and its duration so it can be enforced consistently and reviewed if you appeal. If you believe a ban was made in error, you can reach out to us through our Discord server.

6. Data Retention

We keep account and gameplay data for as long as your account is active, or as long as we need it for legitimate purposes like fraud prevention, security, or enforcing bans. Inactive accounts and their associated data may be deleted or anonymized periodically.

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We don't sell your personal data to third parties for advertising. We do share necessary data with:

  • PlayFab / Microsoft — our backend hosting and data management provider.
  • Platform store providers (for example, Meta) — for account authentication and purchase processing.
  • Discord — only if you choose to join our community server, which runs under Discord's own privacy policy and is entirely separate from in-game data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our games are not knowingly directed at children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your region). If we learn that we've inadvertently collected data from a child without appropriate consent, we'll take steps to delete it.

9. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. To make a request, reach out to us through our Discord server and we'll do our best to help.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our games and tools change. Any updates will be posted here with a new "last updated" date above.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data? The fastest way to reach us is through our Discord server.
